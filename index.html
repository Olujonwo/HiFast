How do I Fund my wallet?
+ -

To fund your wallet, simply click on the fund wallet on the homescreen of the app, choose fund with PeCash( our secured p2p platform), follow the steps and in a few minutes your wallet will be funded with your required amount.

How do I buy my first asset?
+ -

Simply click on the asset you would like to buy, click on buy, input the amount and in a few seconds your fiat currency would be converted to the selected asset.

How do I sell my first asset?
+ -

Simply click on the asset you would like to Sell, click on Sell, input the amount and in a few seconds your cryptocurrency would be converted to the selected Fiat.
